# irFFB2026

Enhanced Force Feedback for iRacing – a major rewrite and evolution of the original irFFB.<br>
You are probably thinking, "irFFB2026?  Wat?  irFFB is so obsolete and dead."  Well, sadly the FFB experience in iRacing still lacks in feeling connected to the car.  The previous versions of irFFB, including irFFB2022, made all of the correct motions for oversteer and understeer but still the effects were lacking.  Although as your hands get more sensitive to the wheel, you start to realize that those FFB effects were not connected to the tire or the track.<br>

irFFB2026's fills this gap utilizing an industry wide recognized tire model called the Pacejka Magic Formula.  The Pacejka Magic Formula includes components, such as Self Aligning Torque, that has been used in irFFB2026 to create that feeling of being connected to the tires.  At the end of the day, our only connection to the tires is through the steering wheel.  

### Original Upstream
Built upon and conceptually forked from the original work by **nlp80**  
Original repository: https://github.com/nlp80/irFFB  
(Added as remote 'upstream' in this repo for history and reference)

All foundational credit goes to nlp80 and early contributors.  
This project diverged significantly starting with irFFB2022 and has been fully rewritten in irFFB2026 with Pacejka Magic Formula SAT + vertical effects, low-latency Game modes, Auto Tune, SimHub integration, and more.

Jetseat, Fans, and HiGuardian have been removed.
